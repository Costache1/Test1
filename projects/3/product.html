Titanwolf Keyboards

MECHANICAL GAMING KEYBOARD

Developed for the fastest games. The Titanwolf INVADER lets you react without delay and gives you the decisive advantage in shooters, MMORPGs or RTS games. The built -in Kailh Blue switches stand for excellent durability and incredibly fast response. There is also an anti-ghosting function , which allows you to use several keys at the same time. The reason for this is the independent control of the respective switches. In order to give you a visual advantage, the INVADER is equipped with Titanwolf RGB backlighting. Set your own personal keyboard lighting or use one of the pre-installed effects to stand out from the crowd. The possibilities are endless.
